Our Services

End-to-end LPG supply chain solutions, from importation and storage to cross-border distribution across the SADC region.

Bulk LPG Supply

We provide bulk LPG directly to wholesalers, distributors and large-scale industrial consumers across Southern Africa. Our supply model ensures continuity even in high-demand periods through strategic partnerships and inventory management.

  • Direct sourcing from refineries and international importers
  • Supply to wholesalers and industrial consumers at scale
  • Consistent product quality with ISO-certified procedures
  • Flexible volume arrangements tailored to customer requirements

LPG Importation & Trading

We manage the entire LPG importation process through South African ports and inland terminals. Our trading desk coordinates procurement schedules and shipping logistics to minimize delays and maximize supply reliability.

  • Import management through South African ports and terminals
  • Procurement scheduling and volume planning
  • Coordination with international shipping and logistics partners
  • Compliance with import regulations and documentation

Cross-Border Logistics & Distribution

Our logistics team handles the full inland transportation and cross-border delivery process for LPG shipments across SADC member states. We ensure seamless, compliant delivery from terminal to final destination.

  • Inland bulk LPG transportation by specialized road tanker fleet
  • Customs clearance and cross-border compliance management
  • Coordination across Malawi, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Mozambique, Botswana & Tanzania
  • Real-time shipment tracking and delivery scheduling

Supply Chain & Market Support

We provide strategic supply chain advisory and market development support to our clients and distribution partners. Our regional expertise enables us to develop tailored operational plans that improve market access and profitability.

  • Market expansion strategy and regional opportunity analysis
  • Supply forecasting and demand planning services
  • Competitive pricing strategies for regional LPG markets
  • Operational planning tailored to regional market conditions

Storage & Inventory Optimization

Our 50-ton LPG storage plant in Lilongwe, Malawi serves as the operational hub for our distribution network. We implement strategic inventory management practices to ensure product is always available when and where it is needed.

  • 50-ton capacity LPG storage plant in Lilongwe, Malawi
  • Strategic stock management to prevent supply disruptions
  • Safety-compliant storage facilities with routine inspections
  • Inventory optimization systems for cost-effective operations
