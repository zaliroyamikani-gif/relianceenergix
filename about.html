Who We Are

Our Story

Reliance Internationer Africa Oil & Gas is an Africa-based bulk LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) supplier and energy solutions provider headquartered in Lilongwe, Malawi.

We specialize in the importation, storage, and cross-border distribution of LPG across the SADC (Southern African Development Community) region. Our operations span Zimbabwe, Zambia, Malawi, Mozambique, Botswana and Tanzania, ensuring that energy reaches where it is needed most.

At our core facility, we operate a 50-ton LPG storage plant that forms the backbone of our supply operations. From this hub, we coordinate logistics, manage inventory, and ensure continuity of supply to a wide customer base across the region.

Our company tagline, Plan. Operate. Maintain. Optimize., reflects our disciplined, process-driven approach to energy delivery.

Quality, Health,
Safety & Environment

Reliance Internationer Africa is committed to maintaining an ISO 9001:2000 certified Quality Management System. Our QHSE policy embeds safety and environmental responsibility at every stage of our operations.

Our management team takes personal responsibility for creating a work environment where safety is never compromised. Every employee is trained, empowered and accountable for upholding our QHSE standards.

  • Compliance with all relevant national and international safety regulations
  • Zero-harm culture across all operational sites
  • Regular staff safety training and hazard assessments
  • Environmental impact minimization at storage and distribution points
  • Continuous quality improvement through documented management systems
  • Transparent reporting and stakeholder accountability
