Why LPG?

LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) is one of the most versatile and efficient energy sources available. It burns cleanly, is easy to store and transport, and is far lower in carbon emissions compared to traditional solid fuels such as wood and coal, making it the ideal transition fuel for Africa's energy needs.

Across the SADC region, millions of homes and businesses rely on LPG for cooking, heating, industrial processes and power generation. Reliable supply is essential to livelihoods, and that's precisely what Reliance Internationer Africa delivers.
